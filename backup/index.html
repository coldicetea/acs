Remote Backup

Data backups are the single most important task you can perform with your computer system. Unfortunately they are often performed incorrectly, and can result in massive data loss. In order to manage a proper backup, you need a skilled and experienced network engineer to setup a verifiable backup system. At ACS, we take backup very seriously and have created a three pronged backup approach, giving our customers confidence in their ability to restore data.

Our RescueBackup data backup service utilizes three approaches to guarantee the quality and reliability of your backup. You can utilize one service for your protection or maximize protection and recoverability and use all three!

Imaging

ACS’s Imaging solution is the simply the quickest approach to recovery. Our RapidRecovery® system not only backs up your data, but also your operating system and applications, for quick restoration and getting your employees back to work. Bare Metal restores are possible even to dissimilar hardware.

  • Full machine snapshot
  • Rapid Recovery
  • Data, Application & Configuration backup

Critical File Backup

Our file backup system creates a master copy of all your critical files. Then on a nightly basis, it backs up all the changed files (delta’s). Our system keeps up to 30 days of versions of each file, allowing recovery for files that get corrupted.

  • Multiple Versioning
  • Automatic Backup
  • Daily Reporting
  • Planning & Testing

Offsite Backup

Our Offsite backup system backs up your data to our fully redundant data storage facility. Your data is encrypted with military-grade 448 Blowfish encryption prior to transport to our facility, this ensures your compliance with regulations like HIPAA.

  • Incrediably Fast backups – up to 5GB/min
  • Military-grade encryption
  • Supports Windows, Mac OSX, Linux
  • Virtualization support
  • HIPAA compliant
  • Up to Blowfish 448
  • Additional Onsite Storage
