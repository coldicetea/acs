Email Archiving

Depending on the nature of your organization, you may be required to keep a record of all communications to comply with laws such as HIPAA, SOX and GLBA. Even if you are not required by law, email serves an important communications link between an organizations suppliers and customers. Often during email communications, important information, pricing and promises to customers are relayed from an organizations employees. In order for management to get the full picture of what was promised and what was discussed, access to email communications may be necessary. Email archiving gives your organization the ability to view sent and received email from all of your employees to get a full picture of what has been communicated. Some organizations in the legal and financial industries require archiving for governmental compliance. ACS offers several methods of archiving, including hosted and on premise solutions.

  • Unlimited retention period
  • Unlimited disk space
  • Full indexing
  • Daily backup
  • Instant search & Restore
  • Tamper-proof email
  • Optional Instant Messaging archiving
  • Optional integration with Outlook
